About

I lead the technology that turns ambitious concepts into experiences people remember.

For seventeen years I've worked in the rooms where entertainment technology either works perfectly — or fails in front of a live audience. That pressure shaped how I lead: rigorous on the engineering, calm on delivery, and relentless about the guest experience the systems ultimately serve.

Today, based in Riyadh with JASARA Program Management Company, I direct integrated show systems, AV and lighting across some of the most ambitious entertainment destinations being built anywhere in the world. My focus sits where technical design, program management and leadership meet — aligning designers, contractors and operators around a single, deliverable vision.

The path here ran through the UAE, Hungary, and the demanding, no-excuses world of cruise-ship show operations. It's a career built on one principle: the technology should disappear, and the experience should be all anyone remembers.
