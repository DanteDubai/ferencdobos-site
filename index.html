01 — About

I lead the technology that turns ambitious concepts into experiences people remember — where the engineering has to be flawless and the moment can't be redone.

For seventeen years I've worked in the rooms where entertainment technology either performs perfectly or fails in front of a live audience. That pressure shaped how I lead: rigorous on the engineering, calm on delivery, and relentless about the experience the systems exist to serve.

Today, in Riyadh with JASARA Program Management Company, I direct integrated show systems, AV and lighting across some of the most ambitious entertainment destinations being built anywhere — operating where technical design, program management and leadership converge, aligning designers, contractors and operators around one deliverable vision.

The path here ran through the UAE, Hungary, and the no-second-take world of cruise-ship show operations. One principle throughout: the technology should disappear, and the experience should be all anyone remembers.
